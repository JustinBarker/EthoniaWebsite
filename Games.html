Ethonia

The game that started it all!

This game was similar to League Of Legends, in that it was going to be multiplayer 5v5 and you would play 1 of 5 classes(if I remember right). You would then go into dungeons and kill the Nauklin for loot (instead of buying it) and when your team felt comfortable would then go attack the other team. Or fight over specific dungeons for loot.

The game was designed to have a few different level dungeons, with multiples of each level, but with different loot drops in each. And unique bosses that each would have the best loot of any given type

When I find my game design documents I will post them here
